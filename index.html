Welcome to Pro Wrestling Tournament

About:

This is a simulation of Wrestler Objects that will attack each other once every round.
The Wrestler with the most health at the end of the round will be declared the winner.
The Tournament will continue until there is only one Wrestler left standing.
The "Autoplay" option will skip through the entire Tournament
and just display the winner with the fight history. (the boring version)
Click the "Start Tournament" button to begin
May the best Wrestler Win. Good Luck!
